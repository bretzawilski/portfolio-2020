Bret J. Zawilski, Ph.D. | Writer, Artist, Scholar

Welcome to my portfolio. I'm currently serving as a Lecturer of Critical Skills at Maynooth University in Dublin, Ireland. This page is designed to showcase my academic, professional, and personal projects.

Honestly, this portfolio is a space to pull together my professional and personal interests. The strength of portfoliosis being able to provide a holistic view of an individual, rather than just listing off a series of accomplishments and skills. I'm proud of the work and growth I'm undertaking through each of these pursuits, and ultimately I'm hoping to step more firmly into the creative realm. I write, I think, I argue, and I create. Each of these things are a part of how I see myself nad how I hope to develop over the next years. As with every other human being, growth is continual and I'm not looking to stagnate. Broadly my goal is to inspire critical thinking among the students I work with, but also to speak more broadly to systemic issues in our society through a combination of scholarship, writing, and art. Each of those is a noble endeavor.

I also don't know what to say or what tone to take in all of this. It's so abstract at times. What do people write on their portfolios? What can I steal?

Coordinator of the Critical Skills Digital Symposium

The Digital Symposium is an end-of-year capstone project intended to ask students to draw upon the critical literacies they have developed throughout their two semesters of enrollment in Critical Skills. During the Digital Symposiums first implementation in 2020, the Covid-19 crisis resulted in a sudden shift to remote learning. This shift resulted in the creation of significant online resources intended to help students successfully develop an audio podcast building upon previous research they had gathered.

Additional details can be found on the Critical Skills Podcast Resource Page.
